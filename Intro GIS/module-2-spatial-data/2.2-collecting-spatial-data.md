# Creating & Collecting Spatial Data

## **Data websites:**

**Data.gov** – U.S. open data portal with geospatial datasets.

**USGS Geospatial Data Sources** – Links to _The National Map_, _EarthExplorer_, _GloVis_ (satellite & aerial imagery, elevation, land cover).

**NASA Open Data Portal** - [https://data.nasa.gov/dataset/](https://data.nasa.gov/dataset/)

**OpenStreetMap (OSM)** – Crowd-sourced global mapping data you can export for GIS use.&#x20;

**Natural Earth Data** – Public domain vector & raster world map datasets.&#x20;
